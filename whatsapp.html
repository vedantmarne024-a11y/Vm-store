Description

With WhatsApp on the desktop, you can seamlessly sync all of your chats to your computer so that you can chat on whatever device is most convenient for you.

Stay connected with friends, family, and colleagues through an immersive desktop experience built specifically for Windows.

Key Features:
• End-to-End Encryption: Your personal messages and calls are secured. Nobody outside of your chats, not even WhatsApp, can read or listen to them.
• High-Quality Calling: Make free voice and video calls directly from your desktop, making it easier to collaborate and connect.
• File Sharing: Easily send documents, slideshows, spreadsheets, and media up to 2GB in size.
• Keyboard Shortcuts: Work faster with dedicated desktop shortcuts and native Windows notifications.
