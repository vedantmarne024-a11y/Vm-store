Description

The Minecraft Launcher is your one-stop hub for all things Minecraft on Windows. Whether you're playing the cross-platform Bedrock Edition, the classic Java Edition, or battling it out in Minecraft Dungeons, this launcher connects you to your favorite blocky worlds instantly.

BUILD YOUR WORLD
Prepare for an adventure of limitless possibilities as you build, mine, battle mobs, and explore the ever-changing Minecraft landscape. Survive the night or build a work of art—the choice is yours.

CROSS-PLATFORM PLAY
With the Bedrock Edition, play with friends across consoles, mobile, and PC. Explore massive multiplayer servers, unique mini-games, and discover new community creations in the Marketplace.

• Minecraft: Bedrock Edition for Windows

• Minecraft Legends
